# Gita Learning App - Logo Design Specifications

## 🎨 Logo Concept Options

### Option 1: "Lotus of Knowledge" (Primary Recommendation)
**Design:**
- A stylized lotus flower with 5 petals (representing the 5 Yoga paths)
- Center: ॐ (Om symbol) in gold/saffron
- Each petal in gradient colors:
  - Petal 1 (Karma Yoga): 🌿 Green
  - Petal 2 (Bhakti Yoga): 🔥 Red/Orange
  - Petal 3 (Jnana Yoga): 🧠 Blue
  - Petal 4 (Dhyana Yoga): 🌬️ Purple
  - Petal 5 (Moksha): 🌺 Pink/White
- Background: Subtle mandala pattern or clean white

**Why It Works:**
- Lotus = spiritual growth, enlightenment
- 5 petals = 5 Yoga levels
- Om symbol = universal spiritual symbol
- Colorful = engaging for users
- Recognizable = memorable

---

### Option 2: "Sacred Triangle + Books" (Alternative)
**Design:**
- Upward triangle (Sri Yantra element) representing spiritual ascent
- 3 lines inside = open book pages
- Colors: Saffron (top), White (middle), Green (bottom) = Indian flag colors
- Modern, geometric, minimalist

**Why It Works:**
- Triangle = spiritual progression
- Book pages = learning/knowledge
- Clean and professional
- Easy to scale to favicon size

---

### Option 3: "Chakra with Books" (Modern Approach)
**Design:**
- Circular chakra design with rotating rings
- Center: open book with leaf (wisdom)
- 7 rings representing different energy levels
- Gradient from warm (bottom) to cool (top) colors
- Modern, tech-forward look

**Why It Works:**
- Chakras = spiritual progress
- Books = learning
- Contemporary design
- Good for app icon

## 📐 Technical Specifications

### For App Icon:
- Size: 1024x1024 px (minimum)
- Format: PNG with transparency
- Variations: Full color, monochrome, icon-only
- Padding: 10% safe zone around design

### For App Store:
- iOS App Icon: 1024x1024 px
- Android App Icon: 192x192 px (round), 512x512 px (adaptive)
- Google Play: 512x512 px
- Splash Screen: 2048x2048 px

### Color Palette:
```
Primary Colors:
- Saffron: #FF9933 (Karma Yoga - action)
- Red: #E74C3C (Bhakti Yoga - devotion)
- Blue: #3498DB (Jnana Yoga - knowledge)
- Purple: #9B59B6 (Dhyana Yoga - meditation)
- Gold: #F39C12 (Om center/Moksha)

Secondary:
- White: #FFFFFF (background)
- Dark: #2C3E50 (text/outline)
- Light Gray: #ECF0F1 (subtle elements)
```

## 🎯 Design Elements to Include

✅ **Must Have:**
- Spiritual symbolism (Om, Lotus, or Chakra)
- Connection to Gita/Hindu philosophy
- Modern yet timeless
- Clear and recognizable at small sizes
- Works in color AND black/white

✅ **Nice to Have:**
- 5 elements (5 Yoga levels)
- Book/Learning element
- Ascending progression symbol
- Gradient or depth effect

❌ **Avoid:**
- Too complex (hard to scale down)
- Political symbols
- Offensive religious interpretations
- Similar to existing app logos
